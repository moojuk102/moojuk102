### [TheI](https://github.com/moojuk102/TheI)

A dice-based RPG built with **Unity** and **C#**. Players collect and customize dice, build parties of characters, and battle enemies in a turn-based combat system.

**Key features:**

- **Dice mechanics** — throw, skin, reinforce, and equip dice with unique abilities
- **Turn-based battles** — characters and enemies take turns on tile-based maps with skill and camera choreography
- **Party & character management** — recruit heroes, assign equipment, and organize parties
- **Stage progression** — multiple chapters and stages with distinct maps and enemy encounters
- **Data persistence** — local save/load system for user profiles, inventories, and party configurations
- **Manager architecture** — modular singleton managers (Game, Data, Play, Screen, Sound, Popup, Map, Skill, ObjectPool) for clean separation of concerns
